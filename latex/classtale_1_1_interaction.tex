\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an \mbox{\hyperlink{classtale_1_1_interaction}{Interaction}} an \mbox{\hyperlink{classtale_1_1_actor}{Actor}} had with other \mbox{\hyperlink{classtale_1_1_actor}{Actors }}. 

Extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classtale_1_1_kernel}{Kernel}} class, so it has to hold every information that is necessary to recreate the causal chain leading to this Event.
